Cliffcoast

A mystical settlement in a fantastical world

Background Flavor

Above the village is a tower that once housed a literal math wizard. He enforced numbering everything, and about 75% of the citizens still have numerical names. It's a rare village with a grid street layout and numbered addresses. The math wizard was defeated by big dumb orcs a long time ago and no one remembers what the name of the town refers to.

Settlement Features

Cliffcoast is a large city 10,001-25,000 located in the river region of the areas greater grassland. The settlement seems to be new. Cliffcoast and the local surroundings are under the control of the theocrats.
