Wyverntown

A mystical settlement in a fantastical world

Background Flavor

In the center of this village is a perpetual flame spouting from a hole in the ground, which is actually the location of the original well. Below the town is a pocket of gas that's mostly methane. Old Jim Straw lit his pipe at the well 30 years ago and cause an explosion (he was never seen again). The villagers make use of the fire to warm themselves and cook.

Settlement Features

Wyverntown is a large town 2,001-5,000 located in the foot hills region of the areas greater temperate deciduous forest. The settlement seems to be new. Wyverntown and the local surroundings are under the control of the plutocrats.
